Taking Fearless Action – My Commitment Contract

  • I commit to identifying the performance cues that will help me concentrate my best.
  • I commit to immersing myself in my performance cues.
  • I commit to focusing on what I can control and not letting what I cannot control affect my confidence.
  • I commit to quickly recognizing distractions and refocusing when necessary.
  • I commit to identifying both internal and external distractions and making adjustments to my focus as needed.
  • I commit to using the Three R’s strategy to help me refocus on my performance cues.
  • I commit to entering the roll of the focused athlete before I compete.
  • I commit to using my pre-game routine to help me focus on my game.
  • I commit to focusing on my pre-shot or pre-performance routine before I execute a skill or routine.
  • I commit to recognizing when my routine breaks down and making an adjustment to my focus.
  • I commit to using the Focused Athlete Test to evaluate my concentration skills after competition.
  • I commit to using the Focused Athlete Mental Imagery Program.
  • I commit to being prepared to handle any distraction that might happen to me during competition.
  • I commit to keeping my mind focused in the present moment, and never think about the past or future when I am executing a play, performing a routine, or in the middle of a race.
