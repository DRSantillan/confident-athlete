The first part is the ability to understand the mental strategies that will help improve your mental game. The second stage is the ability to apply your mental strategies to your sport daily. The third and final stage is your ability to commit, over the long term – months and months, to improve your focus and mental attitude. Your learning is never complete without the diligence to stay committed to your mental training program.
