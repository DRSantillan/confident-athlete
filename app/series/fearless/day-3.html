Day 3 - Fearless Strategy

Being a perfectionist is not all bad. Some behaviors are very functional and actually helpful to your success in sports and life!

So the first step is to identify what behaviors are helpful and promote those traits by keeping them in action! But you also need to identify the behaviors that cause you to feel anxiety, tension, fear, or try too hard in sports, as these behaviors lead to problems in competition.
