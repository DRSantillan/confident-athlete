Taking Fearless Action – My Commitment Contract

  • I commit to learning to perform fearlessly without tension or anxiety.
  • I commit to choosing to accept that I am human and will make mistakes.
  • I commit to striving for perfection, but know I cannot be perfect.
  • I commit to focusing on my goals, not what I assume others want for me.
  • I commit to identify my beliefs that are not functional to performing my best.
  • I commit to discarding strict expectations, and performing with confidence.
  • I commit to replace expectations with small process goals.
  • I commit to being my own best coach and supporting myself.
  • I commit to only maintain positive self-talk, and stop my negative self-talk.
  • I commit to implementing positive strategies to overcome my hot buttons.
  • I commit to focus on what I want, not fear what I fear will happen.
  • I commit to using process goals to help me focus on positive execution.
  • I commit to having self-acceptance, and I am not dependent on others for it.
  • I commit to adjusting my mindset when I focus too much on avoiding errors.
  • I commit to trusting in my training, and compete free of tension.
  • I commit to learning to let go of the practice mindset and perform my best!
  • I commit to performing with a functional mindset in competition.
  • I commit to training myself in practice, and being a performer in competition.
  • I commit to simplifying my performance in competition.
  • I commit to practice like I perform so I can perform my best in competition.
  • I commit to define myself as a person first, one who happens to play sports.
  • I commit to striving for balance in my life when not involved my sports.
  • I commit to leaving my performance on the playing field.
