Welcome to The Fearless Athlete: A 14-day Plan for Unbeatable Trust.

Fear of failure and perfectionism is so prevalent in my work with athletes that it is becoming rampant in athletics. What you may not know or understand is that athletes with fear of failure are highly motivated individuals who want badly to succeed and reach their goals. Perfectionists are incredibly motivated to improve and succeed. But this very positive mindset to use in practice can actually hinder them and undermine perfectionists from reaching peak performance in sports.

Fearless Athlete teaches you the same strategies I teach my personal coaching students when working with athletes in the area of fear of failure, perfectionism, and trust in sports. By the end of this 14-day program, you will have learned the key mental strategies to help you perform with freedom, fearlessness, and unbeatable trust in virtually any competitive situation!

Many of the athletes I work with focus too much on having the perfect performance or fixate on how they will appear to others (such as teammates, parents, coaches) when performing. This hidden need to execute flawlessly or to look good in competition undermines performance during competitive situations. In this workbook, you will be asked to look honestly at yourself to discover the beliefs and attitudes that keep you stuck in a comfort zone (or a certain performance level) and hold you back from reaching your goals.

Throughout the next 14 days of this workbook, I will present many self-awareness exercises to help you pinpoint your own fears and attitudes that block your success. Are you ready to learn unbeatable trust? Great! Letâ€™s get started right now!

How to use this program

Each day you will be given specific tasks to complete to identify mental barriers and improve your mental game. At times you will be given awareness exercises to help you identify mindsets that undermine or alternately, contribute to success in sports. At the end of 14 days, you will be well on your way to unbeatable trust.

Each day of the program is divided into daily tasks which you need to complete. For maximum benefit from each Daily Fearless Strategy, you should complete all the tasks, starting with Day 1 and work your way to Day 14. You may choose to complete the workbook/audio in 7 days if you do 2 days at a time.

Use the audio program that accompanies The Fearless Athlete to help guide you through the program. For example, open the workbook to Day 1 and listen to the audio for Day 1. After you complete the audio for Day 1, stop and complete the workbook exercises for Day 1 before proceeding to Day 2 audio.
