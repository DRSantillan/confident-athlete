Day 14 - Audio

Congratulations! Way to go! You should pat yourself on the back for completing The Fearless Athlete program. The new, fearless you is on your way to unbeatable trust. You should be proud of yourself for taking action with your mental game and recognizing that you can improve your attitude and mental toughness. You will be rewarded for taking action to improve your mental game! Now for the real fun part…

I need you to take fearless action now! You are armed with the powerful mental strategies and tools to help you remove fear, focus on success, and improve your trust, which is an excellent first step! But, this is just the beginning to rocket you to unbeatable trust! THE most important objective is to use the lessons in this workbook and CD program and apply them daily to your practice and play! This is critical to achieving peak performance…

I make sure all my students understand that learning any skills, especially mental game skills, requires three steps. The first part is the ability to understand the mental strategies that will help improve your mental game. The second stage is the ability to apply your mental strategies to your sport daily. The third and final stage is your ability to commit, over the long term – months and months, to improve your trust, confidence, and mental attitude. Learning is never complete without the diligence to stay committed to your mental training program.

My suggestion is to review The Fearless Athlete at least once a month and more often if necessary. Use the “Assess Your Progress” sections at the end of each session to help you identify areas to improve and track your progress. On the following page, I have included a summary of the action plans for each day of the 14-day program that will help you do a quick review of the program. Remove this page from your workbook and post it where you can see it every day. You should review these action plans often and work daily to apply 1-2 of the concepts to your practice, training, and competition! On the following page, you will find the final Taking Fearless Action commitment contract that you should review and sign now. Warning: if you sign the contract, be prepared to take massive action!
