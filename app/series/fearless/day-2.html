Day 2 - Warm-up

I work with many athletes who have perfectionistic behaviors or tendencies. I don’t know why this is… Do some sports instill perfectionism? Some traits of perfectionism are very helpful to improving your performance as I discuss in Day #3. However, my experience shows that fear of failure is closely related to perfectionism in sports. Athletes who are perfectionistic (and highly motivated) do not perform up to their potential because of fear of failure.

It is ironic that the athlete to want to win the most, a train the most are often those who are the most likely to be mentally stuck in fear of failure. perfectionistic athletes often lose sight of the real golden sports–To use training to perform well in competition.

They become so engrossed in training and practice that perfecting their technique or working hard in practice becomes a primary goal instead of performing with confidence in competition. And when they do compete, they believe in anything but perfect execution or winning is deemed as failure.
