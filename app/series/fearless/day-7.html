next the best way to do this is to set small process goals each competition. These help you focus on what to do instead of what not to do. They used to types of process goals to help athletes focus on what to do:

  • Performance goals: goals based on something you can measure such as number of rebound, block shops, or fairways hit the regulation.
  • Mental goals: goals based on your mental game and help you focus on one play, shot, or routine at a time, such as committing to a plane or visualising your pitch before you start the wind up.
