Taking Fearless Action – My Commitment Contract

  • I commit to focusing on what I can control and not letting what I cannot control affect my confidence.
  • I commit to quickly recognizing and recording any doubts I ponder about my sport so I can make adjustments in my thinking.
  • I commit to reviewing and adding to my confidence resume everyday by recording my strengths and accomplishments.
  • I commit to being proactive with my self-confidence before I enter sports arena.
  • I commit to using my pregame routine to set the stage for confidence.
  • I commit to developing a healthy philosophy and beliefs that support my self-confidence.
  • I commit to abolishing perfectionistic traits that suck the life out of my confidence.
  • I commit to viewing my “athletic self” with self-descriptors or labels.
  • I commit to applying my self-talk statements for ultimate self-confidence.
  • I commit to crushing my doubts quickly by using my reframing strategy.
  • I commit to preparing my mind to cope with any challenge I will face.
  • I commit to using the Three R’s to refocus after errors and to never dwelling on the past.
  • I commit to being an intimidator instead of being intimidated by others.
  • I commit to practicing my coping strategies before I begin to feel irritated or frustrated about my sport.
