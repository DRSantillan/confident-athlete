Congratulations! You have completed The Confident Athlete. The new, confident you is on your way to ultimate self-confidence. You should be proud of yourself for taking action with your mental game to be the most confident athlete you can be.

However, I need you to take more action now; MASSIVE action right now. You are armed with the information and tools to mold your confidence and take your mental game to a new level. But, this is just the beginning, a new beginning for you to rocket your confidence to new heights.

I tell all my mental coaching students that learning is a three part process. The first part is the ability to understand the mental strategies that will help improve your mental game. The second stage is the ability to apply your mental strategies to your sport daily.

The third and final stage is your ability to commit, over the long haul, to improving your confidence and mental attitude. Your learning is never complete without the diligence to stay committed to your mental training program.

My suggestion is to review The Confident Athlete at least once a month and more often if necessary. Use the confidence scoreboard at the end of each section to help test your ability to apply the concepts after every competition.
