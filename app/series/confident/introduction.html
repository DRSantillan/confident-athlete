A 14-Day Plan for Ultimate Self- Confidence.

Self-confidence is THE NUMBER ONE ASSET that separates great athletes from the rest of the pack. Athletes CANNOT reach true athletic potential without massive amounts of confidence in their game(s) and abilities. Most athletes who seek my services do so because they desire to know how to improve confidence; have a “fragile” state of confidence; or their confidence has been challenged by a difficult experience or encounter.

The Confident Athlete represents everything I teach my individual students and teams to help them improve and develop massive amounts of confidence quickly and maintain it over the long term. I share all of my insights, hold nothing back and teach my system for learning ultimate self-confidence to any athlete serious about improving his or her confidence!

“A motto and philosophy that I have really stressed is [SIC];; you’ve got to believe in yourself. Self confidence is without a doubt the first step to success.” ~ Tommy Lasorda, Baseball Manager, Hall of Fame

Confidence is important for every athlete. However, understanding how you can develop confidence daily is the most relevant approach to boosting your confidence. What I have learned through working with athletes at all levels is that athletes must take responsibility for improving confidence just like they work at developing a perfect backhand, pitch, swing, stroke and so forth. Athletes must also have a clear understanding about the beliefs, thoughts, and expectations that consistently undermine confidence.

Confidence development is a two part package. In one respect, athletes need to focus on their strengths and maintain confident-enhancing thoughts about their sport even in the face of adversity. And, in another respect, athletes must understand how to identify the beliefs, thoughts or expectations that undermine or hold back their confidence.

How to use this program

Each day you are given specific tasks to complete to help you build confidence. At the end of 14 days, you should be on your way to ultimate confidence, but it is important to keep up the strategies I share with you far beyond 14 days to gain the most benefit from the program.

Each day of the 14-day program is divided into sections of various tasks which you should complete. To gain the most confidence from each daily booster, you should complete all the tasks each day.

In addition to the daily tasks, use the audio program that came with The Confident Athlete to help guide you through your confidence program each day. Listen to the audio for Day 1 and then complete the workbook exercises before proceeding to the Day 2 audio.
